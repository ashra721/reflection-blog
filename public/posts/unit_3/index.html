Journey from failure to figuring

April 10, 2026

unit 3 : journey from failure to figuring The name of the unit is “reflecting on failure” which was kind of relatable ok so this unit was a little different for me it was short but helped me learn a lot . We were again divided into groups of five and were asked to read a part of a book and the book it was an amazing book for students . The book was about real incidents of failure and how people figured it out. My experience with the book reading didn’t ended up with just ending up doing the assigned task and contributing for the group .

While I got the book ‘The right kind of wrong:The science of failing well’. I was preparing for one of my test in other course in parallel the test result showed up. The moment I saw the score I was really disheartened it felt like all my hard work got wasted I started blaming myself for everything out there. But then after a while the stories I read from the book poped in my mind saying it ok to fail once but its not ok to accept that hard work is wasted and accepting that I’m a failure, after a few hours I felt read the chapters I’ve not read and I read a couple of pages which made me feel better and got my mind to prepare myself again woth more efforts.

So the third unit was not just doing what we are expected to do before the deadline but was actually about learning to face failure make way and make things work out in a city where you mom is not there to ask if you are doing good, if not then what’s wrong. for time being this is all about see you in the next unit with some new experience.
